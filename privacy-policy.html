Privacy Policy

For Don't Seek on Quest 2 and Quest 3 Family

Don't Seek is a game developed by Seventy 4 Productions for the Meta Quest platform. We prioritize your privacy and are committed to complying with all applicable privacy regulations, including the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We will never request, gather, or transmit any data.

Commitment to Confidentiality

Seventy 4 Productions is committed to safeguarding your privacy while you use Don't Seek on your Meta Quest device. Below are the key points of our privacy policy:

Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" refers to any data or information exchanged between Seventy 4 Productions and the user in any form (though in the context of this game, no such exchange takes place).

Ownership and Use of Data

Any information related to your use of the game will remain entirely private. We do not transmit, sell, or monetize any data, as Don't Seek operates entirely offline. No third-party providers will have access to any information about your gameplay.

Duration of the Agreement

This privacy agreement is effective from the moment you download and install Don't Seek. The terms of this agreement will remain in effect for as long as you use the game, and since no data is collected, your privacy is always maintained.

Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This privacy policy is governed by the laws applicable in your country of residence, and any disputes related to this policy will be handled under the relevant legal jurisdictions.

User Rights & Data Protection

As Don't Seek does collect data through PhotoFab and Photon, this information is not shared with anyone and is kept private.

PlayFab & Photon

PlayFab is used to track player progression, statistics, and account-related game data. No real-world personal information such as real name, email address, or physical address is collected by the app.

Photon is used for multiplayer and server handling. Like PlayFab, nothing within the real-world is contained in Photon.

Data Transmission

There is no data transmission involved in playing Don't Seek. The game is fully offline and does not require or request an internet connection. As a result, no data, including personal or gameplay-related information, is ever transmitted to us or any third parties.

How We Protect Your Privacy

Seventy 4 Productions is dedicated to ensuring that your privacy is respected throughout your experience with Don't Seek. Specifically, we guarantee that:

  • Don't Seek is an online game.
  • No personal information is shared or communicated with any third parties.
  • The game does not log or store any gameplay statistics.
